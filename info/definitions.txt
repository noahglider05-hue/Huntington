Macro-economic data: Stats that reflect an economy's performance; GDP, inflation, unemployment, 
    intrest rates, etc.

ETF: Exchange Traded Fund is an index fund holding a collection of assets and can be traded throughout the 
    day, unlike a mutual fund.

Basket of equities: prepackaged collection of stocks treated as a single unit
